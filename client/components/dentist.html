Our Dentist

Dr. Andrea Victoria Santos, DMD, MSD, FPFA

45 years old

Dr. Andrea Victoria Santos is a highly respected dentist and orthodontist with over 20 years of experience in clinical practice and dental education. After earning her DMD from the University of the Philippines, she pursued advanced orthodontic training at the University of the East and completed her MSD in Orthodontics abroad. She has treated thousands of patients across Metro Manila and Southern Luzon and regularly conducts seminars on modern orthodontic techniques. Her passion for continuous learning and mentoring young dentists sets her apart as a leader in the field.
